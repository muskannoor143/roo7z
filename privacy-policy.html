Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Roo7z ("we", "our", "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it when you use our website and place orders.

1. Information We Collect

  • Identity and contact details: name, email, phone number, shipping address.
  • Order details: items purchased, delivery info, payment status, and support messages.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device data, and basic usage logs.

2. How We Use Data

  • To process orders and provide customer support.
  • To improve product listings and website performance.
  • To send order updates and important account notices.
  • To detect abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access.

3. Cookies

We may use cookies and local storage to keep sessions active, remember preferences, and improve user experience. You can disable cookies in browser settings, but some features may not work correctly.

4. Payments

Payments are processed through third-party providers. Roo7z does not store complete card credentials on its own servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information is shared only with required service providers (such as payment and shipping partners) to fulfill your orders.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data. No online method is fully risk-free, but we continuously improve security practices.

7. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of personal data by emailing Contact@roo7z.com.

8. Policy Updates

We may revise this page periodically. Updated versions will be published here with a revised date.
